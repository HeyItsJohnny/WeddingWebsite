Our Story
  • August 3, 2017

    Nancy made the first move.

    It was the summer of '17 and Nancy was looking for some free drinks, and maybe a dinner or two. She downloads an app, and what does she find? A profile with a guy on a hike with his dog.

    Hook, line, and sinker.

  • August 19, 2017

    Johnny made the second move.

    They first met at a 49ers football game, where he goes in for the first kiss. According to Johnny, it was magical. According to Nancy, it wasn't.

    But according to Johnny, it was...

  • August 20, 2017

    First Date

    Johnny takes Nancy to San Francisco where they walk Golden Gate Park, explore the Japanese Gardens, and grab dumplings.

  • October 1, 2017

    Relationship status: changed

    On a sunny afternoon at John Thorton beach, Johnny asks Nancy to change her relationship status on FaceBook.

  • March 11, 2018

    Chunk joins the family.

    Despite Chomper's hesitation, Nancy & Johnny decide to adopt Chunk (Chunkerton Chomp Laroco Tran Jr.) into the family.

  • July 21, 2018

    First wedding as +1s.

    After months of pressure, Nancy finally asks Johnny if he would be her plus 1 (Thanks Christa and Kristin!).

  • December 23, 2018

    Johnny popped the question.

    At the Japanese Gardens where they had their first date, Johnny asks Nancy to marry him.


    SPOLIER ALERT: She said yes.
